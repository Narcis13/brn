# M008: Compound Learning

## Objective

Add recommendation-only compound learning so SUPER_CODEX captures skill health, pattern candidates, roadmap reassessment signals, and process improvement artifacts from completed work.

## Why Now

Phase 7 scales execution, but the control plane still leaves too much hard-won evidence trapped in individual runs. Phase 8 turns those artifacts into durable learning signals without auto-editing source-of-truth docs.

## Exit Criteria

- Completed slices refresh project-skill health, emit deterministic pattern candidates, and write roadmap reassessment reports.
- Postmortems also emit process improvement reports with actionable follow-ups.
- Learning state, schemas, metrics, and doctor coverage are durable and explainable from disk alone.
- Learning remains recommendation-only and does not rewrite roadmap, patterns, or skills automatically.
