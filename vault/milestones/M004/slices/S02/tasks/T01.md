# T01: Route planning units through the conductor

## Objective

Teach next-action synthesis and state transitions how to distinguish planning units from implementation units.

## Why Now

Planning output is unsafe unless the conductor can route it to the right role, persist it as a run, and refuse to treat it as verified implementation work.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Route roadmap and milestone units to `strategist`.
- Route modern slices without valid tasks to `slice-planner`.
- Transition successful planning runs back to `plan` after validation instead of `implement`.
